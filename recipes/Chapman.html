Nigerian ChapmanDrink

Description

Nigerian Chapman is an easy cocktail drink with a nice blend of fruity flavours.

Ingredients

  • Sprite
  • Fanta
  • Grenadine Syrup
  • Sliced Cucumber
  • Sliced Lemon
  • Cubes Ice

Steps

  1. Add ice cubes to a bowl
  2. Add sliced cucumber and lemon
  3. Add Fanta, Sprite and Grenadine Syrup
  4. Mix and Enjoy
